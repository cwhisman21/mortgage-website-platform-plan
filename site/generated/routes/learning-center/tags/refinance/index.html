Loan option guide

Cash-Out Refinance

A cash-out refinance replaces the current first mortgage with a larger new loan and provides eligible proceeds after existing liens and transaction costs are addressed. It changes the financing on the entire mortgage balance, so compare the new rate, term, payment, costs, equity, and use of funds with alternatives.

Loan option guide

Refinance

Refinancing replaces an existing mortgage with a new loan. The decision should begin with a defined goal and a side-by-side comparison of the current loan and proposed loan, including closing costs, term, payment, total interest, and the time required to recover upfront expense.
