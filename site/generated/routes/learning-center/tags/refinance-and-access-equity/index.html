Loan option guide

Cash-Out Refinance

A cash-out refinance replaces the current first mortgage with a larger new loan and provides eligible proceeds after existing liens and transaction costs are addressed. It changes the financing on the entire mortgage balance, so compare the new rate, term, payment, costs, equity, and use of funds with alternatives.
