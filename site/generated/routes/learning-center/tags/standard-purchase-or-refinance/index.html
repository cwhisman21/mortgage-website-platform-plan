Loan option guide

Conventional Loans

Conventional mortgages are not insured or guaranteed by FHA, VA, or USDA. Some conform to standards used by Fannie Mae or Freddie Mac, while others do not. A productive comparison focuses on the applicable loan limit, borrower and property review, mortgage-insurance treatment, pricing, and total cost.
