Missouri mortgage and housing guide

Missouri planning often starts with affordability, FHA fit, taxes, and metro-to-suburban price differences.

Missouri borrowers can use the statewide $437K reference, $2,990/mo scenario, and 3.6 months measure as a starting point rather than a quote or property valuation.
