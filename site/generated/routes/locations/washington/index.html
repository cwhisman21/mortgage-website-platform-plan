Washington mortgage and housing guide

Washington borrowers often compare high-cost pricing, jumbo fit, taxes, and local inventory.

Washington borrowers can use the statewide $900K reference, $6,170/mo scenario, and 3.5 months measure as a starting point rather than a quote or property valuation.

Local tax assumptions vary by city and county Insurance should be reviewed early in the payment scenario The current snapshot is dated 2026-07-10.
