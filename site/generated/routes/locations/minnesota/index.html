Minnesota mortgage and housing guide

Minnesota buyers often compare conventional, FHA, and refinance paths across local tax and inventory differences.

Minnesota borrowers can use the statewide $543K reference, $3,720/mo scenario, and 3.5 months measure as a starting point rather than a quote or property valuation.

Local tax assumptions vary by city and county Insurance assumptions should be confirmed before choosing a path The current snapshot is dated 2026-07-10.
