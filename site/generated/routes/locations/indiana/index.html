Indiana mortgage and housing guide

Indiana buyers often compare affordable price points, inventory timing, FHA options, and conventional paths.

Indiana borrowers can use the statewide $406K reference, $2,780/mo scenario, and 3.6 months measure as a starting point rather than a quote or property valuation.

Local tax assumptions vary by city and county Insurance assumptions should be confirmed before choosing a path The current snapshot is dated 2026-07-10.
