# SEO And Compliance

## SEO Strategy

The site should earn organic traffic by becoming a useful local mortgage publication and dashboard.

Primary SEO assets:

- State mortgage guides.
- City mortgage and housing guides.
- Location-specific product sections on city and state pages.
- Local market news.
- Tax and insurance explainers.
- Calculator pages.
- Loan officer and branch pages.

## Avoid Thin Local Pages

Do not create city pages that only swap the city name.

Each city/state page should include unique:

- Market facts.
- Charts.
- Tables.
- Tax context.
- Insurance context.
- Buyer/refinance scenarios.
- Local FAQs.
- Local loan officers.
- Nearby offices.
- Related articles.
- Internal links.

## Internal Linking

Every reference to a correlated page should be a hyperlink:

- City to state.
- State to city.
- City to related products.
- Product to related state/city pages with matching product modules.
- Article to city/state/product/LO/branch pages.
- Loan officer to service areas and products.
- Branch to nearby cities and team members.

## Compliance Controls

Mortgage content requires strong publishing controls.

Sensitive content:

- Rates and APR.
- Payment examples.
- Qualification claims.
- Product eligibility.
- Down payment assistance.
- State licensing claims.
- Loan officer licensing.
- AI-written local news.

Required controls:

- Clear source tracking.
- Last updated date.
- Reviewer/approver.
- Disclosure attachment.
- Version history.
- Archive history.
- State-specific licensing checks.

## Rate And Payment Content

Any rate/payment module should be designed with disclosure space and data provenance from the start.

Each module should support:

- Assumptions.
- APR/payment explanation.
- Effective date.
- Source.
- Eligibility caveat.
- Link to full disclosures.
