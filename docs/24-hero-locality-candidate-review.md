Ten comparatively useful files are **nominated for rights review only** so their provenance work is not lost; nomination is not acquisition, selection, review, approval, or permission to publish.

The playbook lifecycle is sequential:

1. research candidate;
2. nominated for acquisition and rights review;
3. acquired with a complete rights record;
4. selected only after the preceding gates pass;
5. reviewed and approved before publication can be authorized.

`attribution_label` is only a human-readable credit label. It is not a complete derivative-use recipe. ## Eligibility rules applied

Rights eligibility and editorial-locality eligibility are separate decisions:

- `rights_eligibility` says only whether a source may advance into formal rights review. Unverified people or property permissions remain blocked.
- `editorial_locality_eligibility` says whether the visible place evidence is strong enough even to nominate. Ten scenery-only or visually nonspecific candidates are editorially ineligible with recorded reasons.
- A city candidate that is editorially eligible must have at least three distinct visible locality-signal types; a state candidate must have at least two. Counts in the manifest equal their signal arrays.
- Passing an individual signal threshold does not make a layered local portrait. A valid selected set must also include neighborhood-scale lived behavior and pass manual editorial review, crop review, rights review, and the repetition limits below.
- The failed manual audit governs. No selected candidate or `selected_candidate_id` is allowed while it remains failed.

## Manual set audit

Manual editorial result: **failed**.

The structural thresholds apply to any future selected set:

| Motif gate | Maximum or minimum | Observed among the 10 nominees | Result |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Skyline | Maximum 4 | 9 | Fail |
| Water | Maximum 3 | 7 | Fail |
| Lived behavior | Minimum 4 | 0 | Fail |

The thresholds prevent an individually plausible source from passing when the assembled set is monotonous or unrepresentative. Manual editorial review remains authoritative even when numeric thresholds pass.

The free-stock pass also fails qualitatively:

- state candidates collapse diverse states into scenery;
- city nominees privilege downtown observation over ordinary neighborhood life;
- only one blocked candidate, Boulder Pearl Street, visibly records lived behavior;
- rights-safe emptiness has produced an unacceptably people-free portrait of local mortgage markets;
- repeated water and skyline motifs overstate waterfront and downtown life, especially in Florida.
