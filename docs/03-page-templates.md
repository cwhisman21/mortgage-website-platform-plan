# Page Templates

## Shared Page Pattern

Most public pages should use a shared pattern:

- Contextual hero.
- Sticky CTA rail or mobile bottom CTA.
- Dashboard or snapshot module.
- Educational modules.
- Charts and tables.
- Related loan officers.
- Related branches.
- Related products.
- Related articles/news.
- FAQ.
- Compliance/disclosure block.

## City Page Template

Purpose: local mortgage and housing market hub.

Standard modules:

- Hero with city/state name, primary CTA, and secondary local expert CTA.
- Market snapshot cards.
- Home price trend chart.
- Mortgage payment trend chart.
- Inventory/days-on-market chart.
- Property tax and insurance table.
- Down payment/payment scenario table.
- Nearby city comparison table.
- Buy section.
- Refinance section.
- Selling/move-up section.
- Local loan officer cards.
- Nearby branch cards.
- Related product cards.
- Local news/editorial feed.
- City FAQ.

The page should be dashboard-first with short, useful supporting copy.

## State Page Template

Purpose: parent hub for statewide mortgage guidance.

Standard modules:

- State hero.
- Statewide market snapshot.
- Major city comparison table.
- Property tax and insurance overview.
- State product guidance.
- Statewide loan officer directory.
- Branch directory.
- City links.
- State news feed.
- State FAQ.

## Product Page Template

Purpose: explain a loan product and route users to relevant local help.

Standard modules:

- Product hero.
- Who it fits.
- Requirements overview.
- Benefits and tradeoffs.
- Payment/down-payment examples.
- Product FAQ.
- Related calculators.
- Specialist loan officers.
- Related city/state pages.
- Related articles.

## Location Product Module

Location-specific product content should live as a reusable section on state and city pages, not as a standalone page template.

Examples of modules:

- FHA loans in Texas.
- FHA loans in Austin, TX.
- VA loans in Colorado Springs, CO.
- Jumbo loans in Orange County, CA.
- Refinance in Phoenix, AZ.

These modules should include localized data, local loan officers, local articles, and location-specific FAQ content. They should link to the canonical product page when the user needs full product education.

## Blog Article Template

Purpose: editorial content that feeds SEO and placement.

Standard modules:

- Article title and summary.
- Author/reviewer.
- Publication and update date.
- Related location cards.
- Related product cards.
- Related loan officer cards.
- Related calculators.
- Inline internal links.
- CTA panel.
- Source/disclosure notes where needed.

## Sticky CTA Behavior

Desktop:

- Right-side sticky panel.
- Primary CTA, secondary CTA, and context action.

Mobile:

- Bottom sticky bar.
- Three compact actions maximum.

CTA should adapt by section:

- Market section: Estimate payment.
- Product section: Compare loan options.
- Loan officer section: Contact local expert.
- News section: Get local guidance.
- Refinance section: Check refinance options.
